Data Description

We used the United Nations - Department of Economic and Social Affairs (Population Division's) standard most used dataset. The dataset consisted of continent, sub-region, country, income level, development level data from the year 1950-2101, with the data for the years 2022-2101 forecasted by the UN . We used the data from 1960 - 2020 to train and validate our prediction model and used our model to predict net migration rate for the years 2021-2050.

Model

We trained five different Prediction models namely, Linear Regressor, Lasso Regressor, Ridge Regressor, Random Forest Regressor and Support Vector Regressor (SVR), with our data from 1960 - 2020. The respective Root Mean Squared Errors (RMSE) of the models on the Validation data can be seen in the below table. Since the Linear Regression model has the lowest RMSE, we are using this as the final prediction model on the test data.

The final Linear Regressor model prediced Net Migration rate on the test data with a RMSE of 0.0061.
